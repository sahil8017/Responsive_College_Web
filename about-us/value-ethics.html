Core Values

The Dyal Singh College community is committed to a set of enduring Core Values that guide our Vision and Mission. These values are focused on the sustainable development of the institution and serve as a compass through changing times.

Inspiring Environment

We preserve a serene cultural and aesthetic environment that is welcoming, secure, and well-suited for young scholars.

Value-Based Education

We focus on outcomes, discipline, punctuality, and social justice, striving to inculcate good human values.

Transformational Learning

We promote multi-disciplinary research, creativity, innovation, and critical thinking as a lifelong process.

Continuous Enrichment

We commit to fair, honest practices and utilize feedback for improvement and professional exposure.

Environmental Sustainability

We value and adopt practices to protect the environment for future generations.

Student Centric System

Fostering the professional and personal growth of all students is our primary focus.

Human Values

Basic human values are at the core of being human and include truth, honesty, loyalty, love, and peace. They bring out the fundamental goodness of human beings and society at large.

Importance of Human Values

  • Provide understanding of attitudes, motivation, and behaviors.
  • Influence our perception of the world around us.
  • Represent the interpretation of “right and wrong.”
  • Provide a way to understand humans and organizations.

The five core human values we uphold are: Right Conduct, Peace, Truth, Peaceful Co-existence, and Discipline.

Professional Ethics

For Teachers

Teachers are expected to adhere to a responsible pattern of conduct, seek continuous professional growth, perform their duties with dedication, and deal justly and impartially with students. They should respect students' rights and encourage their development.

For Non-Teaching Staff

Non-teaching staff must adhere to a responsible pattern of conduct, maintain up-to-date records, and operate with transparency and accountability. They are expected to cooperate with colleagues and assist students professionally.
