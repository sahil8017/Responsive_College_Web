Official Logo

The visual symbol of our heritage, values, and enduring legacy since 1910.

तमसो मा ज्योतिर्गमय

(Tamaso Mā Jyotirgamaya)

“From darkness, lead me to the light.”

This profound verse from the Brihadaranyaka Upanishad serves as our guiding principle. It encapsulates our mission to dispel the darkness of ignorance with the light of knowledge, wisdom, and truth, empowering our students to become enlightened and responsible citizens.
