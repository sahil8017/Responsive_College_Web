Online Admissions

Start your journey with us. Follow the steps below to apply online.

Admissions Open 2025-26

All admissions to Dyal Singh College, Karnal are processed through the centralized online admission portal of the Department of Higher Education (DHE), Haryana.

Go to DHE Portal & Apply Now

You will be redirected to the official government website.

Step-by-Step Application Process

1

Register on the Portal

Visit the DHE Haryana portal using the link above. Register yourself with a valid mobile number and email address to generate your applicant ID and password.

2

Fill Application Form

Log in and carefully fill in your personal details, academic qualifications, and other required information in the online application form.

3

Upload Documents

Scan and upload clear copies of your photograph, signature, and all necessary academic documents as specified on the portal.

4

Choose Dyal Singh College

In the college selection section, ensure you select "Dyal Singh College, Karnal" and choose your preferred courses in order of preference.

5

Submit & Wait for Merit List

Review your application, submit the form, and pay any applicable fees. Keep checking the DHE portal and our college website for merit list announcements.
