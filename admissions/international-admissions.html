Application Process

1

Visit the Portal

Click the button above to go to the "Study in India" portal, the one-stop platform for international student admissions in India.

2

Create Your Account

Register on the portal by providing your basic details to create a unique user account.

3

Complete Application Form

Fill out one common application form. Ensure you select "Dyal Singh College, Karnal" during the college selection process.

4

Upload Documents

Upload required academic transcripts, letters of recommendation, and other necessary documents as specified.

5

Receive Admission Offers

If selected, you will receive an admission offer directly through the portal. Accept the offer to confirm your seat.
