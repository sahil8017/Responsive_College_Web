About the Library

The College Library was established in 1949 and has since grown into a vital resource for students and staff. Starting with just 2,108 books, our collection now boasts over 57,795 volumes. We subscribe to numerous national periodicals, journals, and newspapers, providing access to a vast array of e-journals and databases through the UGC-INFLIBNET N-LIST program.

Spanning 2,179.6 sq. mts., the library is fully automated with Koha software and offers a digital section with CDs, DVDs, and high-speed internet terminals.
