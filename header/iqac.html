About IQAC

The prime task of the IQAC is to develop a system for conscious, consistent & catalytic improvement in the overall performance of institutions. It works towards the realization of the goals of quality enhancement & sustenance.

Key Strategies

  • Timely, efficient academic performance.
  • Optimization of modern teaching methods.
  • Credibility of evaluation procedures.
  • Research sharing and networking.

Primary Functions

  • Development of quality benchmarks.
  • Dissemination of information.
  • Organization of workshops.
  • Preparation of AQAR.
